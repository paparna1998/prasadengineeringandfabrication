Storage Tank

Description

Oil and gas industry is the prime vendee of storage tanks for bulk contamination of fluids to perform different stages of the refinery process. Prasad Engineering Manufacture & Supply all types of Vertical and Horizontal Storage Tanks for various industrial applications conforming to APl-650, API-620 standards, etc.

The American Petroleum Standards (API) 650 outlines the minimum requirements of design, fabrication, manufacturing, erection, and inspection of welded storage tanks in various industrial sectors. It helps minimize the risk of product failure and restrict any potential environmental damage. Users specify the standards following their specific needs and requirements
